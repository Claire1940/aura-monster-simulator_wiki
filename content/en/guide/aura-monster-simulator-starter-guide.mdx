<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Aura Farming, Upgrades, & Aura Shop Rolls">

Aura is the lifeblood of your monster's progression. While climbing is the most direct way to farm Aura, optimizing your upgrade path and utilizing the Aura Shop are what separate amateur climbers from legendary monsters.

### Climbing Skill Upgrades Priority

Spend your hard-earned Aura on these stat upgrades in the following priority order:

1. **Movement Speed**: Reduces the time spent on each individual stair step.
2. **Aura Multiplier**: Increases the amount of Aura harvested per step climbed.
3. **Hazard Resistance**: Decreases the knockback and damage taken from high-floor traps.

### Gems & Aura Shop Utility

Gems are your premium currency, primarily obtained through codes or high-floor milestones. Do not spend them blindly. The Aura Shop allows you to roll for different visual and mechanical aura effects.

| Resource | Primary Acquisition | Main Spending Destination | Best Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Aura** | Stair Climbing | Stats & Climbing Upgrades | Spend immediately to maximize climbing efficiency. |
| **Gems** | Promo Codes & Milestones | Aura Shop Rerolls | Roll until you get a decent passive boost, then hoard for late-game. |

<Callout type="success" title="Boost Management">
Always activate your 2x Aura Boosts right before starting an active, uninterrupted climbing session. Avoid idling in menus or customizing your character while a timed booster is ticking down.
</Callout>

### High-Floor Hazards & Obstacle Navigation

As the official game description warns, the higher you climb, the more dangerous the stairs become. To survive higher floors, keep these rules in mind:

- **Observe before moving**: High-floor hazards operate on strict, predictable timers. Watch the pattern before rushing through.
- **Ditch Auto-Climb**: Auto-climbing moves you in a straight line, making you an easy target for falling hazards. Always switch to manual controls in danger zones.
- **Coordinate Rebirths**: Never rebirth right after activating a fresh 2-hour Aura Boost. Wait for your boosts to expire, push to your absolute limit, and then trigger the reset.
