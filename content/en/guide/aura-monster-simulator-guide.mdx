<Section icon={Map} title="Mastering the Endless Staircase: Core Mechanics">

Aura Monster Simulator is a high-intensity Roblox experience centered around the "Endless Staircase" mechanic. Unlike traditional obby games where you reach a static finish line, this simulator challenges you to climb as high as possible while your environment becomes increasingly hostile. The fundamental gameplay loop involves moving upward to generate **Aura**, which serves as the primary currency for physical upgrades. As of the current V1.75 update, the game emphasizes a "Monster" theme where your character's power is visually represented by glowing auras that you unlock via the Aura Shop.

To succeed in the early game, you must understand that climbing is not just about holding the forward button. Every step you take contributes to your Aura total, but the rate of gain is influenced by your current climbing level and active boosts. New players should focus on reaching the first 1,000 steps to unlock basic survival features.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Platform Controls and Movement Optimization">

Navigating the staircase requires different approaches depending on your hardware. The official game description lists specific controls for PC, Controller, and Mobile. High-floor survival depends on your ability to dodge hazards while maintaining an upward trajectory. On PC, the standard WASD layout provides the most precision, while Mobile players must get used to "zone-based" inputs where different parts of the screen control direction.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Aura Farming and Upgrade Priority">

Efficient farming is the difference between a slow grind and rapid progression. Your primary goal is to maximize your "Aura per Step" (APS). This is achieved through a combination of skill upgrades and Aura Shop rolls. In the early game, do not hoard your Aura. Reinvest it immediately into climbing speed and stamina to reach the higher floors where the base Aura rewards are significantly greater.
