<Section icon={Trophy} title="Mastering the Rebirth System">

Rebirth is the most critical mechanic for long-term success. It resets your floor progress and current Aura balance but grants you a permanent multiplier to all future Aura gains. Knowing *when* to Rebirth is the difference between a top-tier player and someone stuck in the mid-game grind.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Push">
    Climb until the cost of the next Climbing Speed upgrade exceeds your total Aura earned in 10 minutes of active play.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Resource Depletion">
    Spend all remaining Aura on the most expensive upgrades possible. Aura does not carry over, so "use it or lose it."
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Boost Check">
    Ensure you don't have a 2x Aura Boost with only a few minutes left. It is better to finish the boost duration before resetting.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Reset">
    Trigger the Rebirth. You will return to Floor 1, but your new multiplier will allow you to reach your previous peak in a fraction of the time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Rebirth Warning">
Rebirthing too early can slow you down if the multiplier isn't high enough to overcome the loss of your current upgrades. Aim for at least a 2x increase in your total multiplier per Rebirth.
</Callout>

</Section>
