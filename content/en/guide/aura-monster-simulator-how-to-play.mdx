<Section icon={Shield} title="High Floor Survival & Hazard Management">

As you climb past floor 1,500, the game shifts from a simple simulator to a survival challenge. The "Monster" aspect of the game becomes more apparent as the stairs become treacherous. Hazards include disappearing steps, swinging pendulums, and "Aura Blasts" that can reset your position by hundreds of floors.

### Hazard Types and Countermeasures

| Hazard Name | Appearance | Effect | How to Avoid |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Void Steps** | Translucent blue steps | You fall through them. | Watch the flicker pattern/jump. |
| **Aura Wind** | Green particles | Pushes you sideways. | Move against the wind direction. |
| **Crumbling Stairs** | Cracked grey steps | Fall after 1 second. | Keep moving; do not stop. |
| **Monster Gaze** | Red glowing eyes | Freezes movement. | Hide behind pillars or jump. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Death Penalty">

</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Rebirth Strategy and Long-term Goals">

Rebirth is the ultimate goal for any serious player. When you Rebirth, you lose your current floor progress and your accumulated Aura, but you gain a permanent **Rebirth Multiplier** and **Gems**. This is the only way to reach the extreme end-game floors (10,000+).

### When Should You Rebirth?

The timing of your Rebirth is critical. If you Rebirth too early, the bonus won't be enough to justify the reset. If you wait too long, you waste time on a slow-climbing run.

| Rebirth Tier | Required Floor | Gem Reward | Recommended Aura Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rebirth 1** | 1,000 | 500 | 1M+ |
| **Rebirth 5** | 5,000 | 2,500 | 50M+ |
| **Rebirth 10** | 15,000 | 10,000 | 500M+ |

<Callout type="info" title="The Rebirth Loop">
After your first Rebirth, you will notice that reaching floor 500 takes half the time. Use this increased speed to farm more Aura than your previous run, allowing you to buy higher-tier upgrades that were previously unaffordable.
</Callout>

As of the **V1.75 update**, Rebirths also unlock exclusive **Titles**. These titles are displayed above your monster's head and provide minor hidden buffs to your climbing efficiency. Collecting all the titles is a primary goal for completionists.
