If you find that the **aura monster simulator controls** are not responding as expected, there are several common fixes. Input lag can be devastating in a game where one wrong turn leads to a massive fall. 

1. **Check Connectivity**: High ping can cause a delay between pressing 'W' and your monster moving.
2. **Rebind Conflicts**: Ensure no other software is overriding your Space bar or Mouse buttons.
3. **Hardware Latency**: Wireless controllers may experience interference; switching to a wired connection is recommended for high-altitude climbing.
4. **Server Updates**: Occasionally, game updates may reset your preferred control scheme. Always verify your settings after a patch.

| Issue | Potential Cause | Recommended Solution |
