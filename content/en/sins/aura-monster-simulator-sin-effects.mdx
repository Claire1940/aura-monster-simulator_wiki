<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Rerolling Your Sin">

Rerolling your Sin requires a steady supply of Gems. To ensure you do not waste resources, follow this optimized sequence to claim free rewards, access the Aura Shop, and roll for the best possible Sin.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Redeem Active Gem Codes">
    Before rolling, click the Codes icon on the left side of the screen. Input active codes like `HAPPYBIRTHDAYDANIEL` (250 Gems) and `500LIKES` (200 Gems) to build your starting Gem pouch.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to the Aura Shop">
    Walk over to the Aura Shop located near the bottom of the endless staircase. This is the central hub where all aura effects and Sin rolls are processed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate the Sin Roll">
    Spend your Gems to roll for a random Sin effect. Each roll completely replaces your current Sin, so ensure you have enough Gems for multiple attempts if you get a low-tier result.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Lock in Your Setup">
    Once you land Pride, Wrath, or Greed, stop rolling. Equipping one of these S-tier Sins will immediately boost your climb, allowing you to save any remaining Gems for future updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>
