### High-Floor Evasion Tactics
1. **Analyze the Pattern**: High-floor hazards operate on fixed timers. Pause briefly on safe platforms to study the rhythm before rushing forward.
2. **Utilize Screen Zones**: If playing on mobile, get comfortable tapping the outer edges of your screen to make sharp, sudden directional adjustments.
3. **Control Your Momentum**: Upgraded speed can make your character slippery. Tap your directional keys gently on narrow steps to avoid overshooting.
