### When to Trigger a Rebirth
Rebirth is a strategic reset. It clears your current climbing upgrades and temporary Aura in exchange for permanent multipliers and progression points. 

- **The Stall Point**: When climbing five additional floors takes longer than your entire initial run, it is time to rebirth.
- **Resource Check**: Ensure you have spent all your current Aura on any remaining cheap upgrades to maximize your final milestone reach before resetting.
- **Post-Rebirth Recovery**: Immediately after resetting, use your permanent multipliers to quickly buy back basic speed upgrades, returning to your previous high-floor range in a fraction of the time.

<Callout type="info" title="Rebirth Preparation">
Never rebirth while a timed 2x Aura Boost is active unless you are certain you can speedrun back to your high-yield farming floors before the boost timer expires.
</Callout>
